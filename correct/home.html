WELCOME TO THE VATICAN MUSEUMS

Welcome to the Vatican Museum, one of the world's most renowned cultural institutions. Established in the early 16th century by Pope Julius II, the museum contains an incredible collection of art and artifacts that span more than 3,000 years of human history. With over 70,000 works on display, including masterpieces by Michelangelo, Raphael, and Caravaggio, visitors can experience a visual journey through the ages. The museum is housed in a complex of buildings that includes the famous Sistine Chapel, with its stunning ceiling painted by Michelangelo, as well as a number of other galleries, courtyards, and gardens. We hope you enjoy your visit and discover the many treasures that the Vatican Museum has to offer!
